Chemistry Textbook

-Attributes
1. subject ("Chemistry")
2. number_of_pages (400)
3. cover_art (False)
4. current_page (200)
5. binding_type (softcover)

-Methods
1. what_subject (returns "Chemistry")
2. percent_complete (returns 50%, based on current_page 200 / number_of_pages 400)
3. whats_on_the_cover (False, nothing on the cover)
4. how_durable (not durable, based on its binding_type)
5. turn_page (current_page now equals 201)
6. draw_on_cover (cover_art now equals True)
7. find_index (current_page now equals number_of_pages at 400)
8. restart_book (current_page now equals 1)
